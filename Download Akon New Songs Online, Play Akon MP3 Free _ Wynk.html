About Akon

Akon rose to prominence in 2004 following the release of "Locked Up", the first single from his debut album Trouble. His second album, Konvicted received three nominations for the Grammy Awards in two categories, Best Contemporary R&B Album for Konvicted album and Best Rap/Sung Collaboration for "Smack That" and "I Wanna Love You".
